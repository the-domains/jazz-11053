So what is Kitcade?

Kitcade is the most amazing Arcade Machine, sent to you in kit form. Our unique design allows you the joy of creating your own machine, with our customisable parts and designs so that you not only get the most perfect machine for you, but you get the fun of creating it yourself too.

All parts are hand-made in the UK. If you don’t have the time to assemble one yourself, we can put it together for you for a small cost.
